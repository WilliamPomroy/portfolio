School project for UI/UX. For working on user testing, prototyping, wireframes and final designs. Everything other than it being about a culinary blog expansion was custom.

June 2020

The first two parts of the project where a wireframe creation and running user tests on people. After the user tests adjusting the wireframe to make up for and adjust to a better user experience from the feedback received. Part three of the project was wireframing and making the final design for the index page of the same site, figuring out what was useful for the site and would help push business. All while optimzing images and making a user friendly design.

When it came to making the final design for the index page of this project, as it was a food blog green healthy colours were what I went with. Using Lato and roboto as the two fonts, Roboto for headings and Lato for body text. Fitting as many images as possible into the page, as the product and presentation is the most important part.
