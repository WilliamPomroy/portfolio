Letters by Cata Co. a small calligraphy and lettering business run in Edmonton. One of the two Capstone Projects for School. A shop built with wordpress and woocommerce.

www.lettersbycataco.com

January 2021 - April 2021

A light colour palette too fit with the target audience set by the client, of female young adults. The grey for the text, and the footer, acting as the one point of contrast.

The fonts chosen were for visibility the client wanted some cursive text which would fit with her business, but would harm the visibility and usablity of the site. We decided on Merriweather for Headings and Arial for body text.

With a website of this size tying all the different elements, styles and pages together was important, keeping the site as unified possible. Using Rounded Corners for the smaller elements of the site, such as buttons, and the sale display for items.

Ease of content editing was important for the project, sheâ€™d need to easily add and change content with her plans to expand in the future. Using Advanced Custom Fields we were able to achieve this. We ended up removing almost all the base functionality of wordpress, such as the block editor, un-enqueuing the base wordpress styles and doing everything custom.

Optimizing the site was an important challenge and the thing that took up the most time, with the amount of bulk added from WordPress, WooCommerce and plugins there was a lot to optimize. With the image optimization, SEO, changing plugin Javascripts and other files. Getting the lighthouse score into the green, and keepig the time to first paint low.
